Chaos Cards Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Chaos Cards

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chaos Cards. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process personal information directly from users.

  • No accounts
  • No login system
  • No user data stored on our servers
  • All gameplay is local to your device

3. Information Collected by Third Parties

While we do not collect personal data ourselves, the app uses third-party advertising services that may collect information automatically.

We use advertising services provided by Google AdMob. These services may collect:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage data

This information may be used to:

  • Display ads
  • Improve ad performance
  • Provide personalized or non-personalized ads

For more information, see Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Tracking Technologies

Third-party services such as AdMob may use tracking technologies including:

  • Cookies
  • Mobile advertising IDs
  • Device identifiers

On iOS devices, users may be asked for permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

5. How Information Is Used

We do not use personal data directly. Third-party services may use automatically collected information to:

  • Serve advertisements
  • Analyze app performance
  • Improve their services

6. Children’s Privacy

Chaos Cards is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party service connected with the app, please contact us and we will review the issue.

7. Your Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA): If you are a California resident, you may have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, subject to applicable law.

European Users (GDPR): If you are located in the EEA, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, and restriction of processing, subject to applicable law.

Because Chaos Cards does not directly collect or store personal information, these rights generally apply to third-party services used within the app.

8. Data Security

We do not store user data on our servers. Gameplay data remains on your device.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated Effective Date.
